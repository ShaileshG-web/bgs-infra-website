Service 01

Structural Strengthening

Aging buildings require expert reinforcement to restore structural integrity and extend service life. BGS Infra specialises in proven techniques that strengthen columns, beams, slabs, and foundations without disrupting occupants.

⚙️

Jacketing

Column and beam jacketing using concrete or steel to increase load-bearing capacity significantly.

🔬

Fibre Wrapping (FRP)

Carbon and glass fibre reinforced polymer wrapping for non-invasive structural strengthening.

💉

Grouting & Injection

Pressure grouting and crack injection to seal structural cracks and restore monolithic integrity.

🧪

Polymer Repair Mortars

High-strength polymer-modified mortars for spalled concrete repair and surface restoration.

Service 02

Waterproofing

Water ingress is one of the leading causes of structural deterioration in Indian buildings. BGS Infra provides comprehensive waterproofing solutions designed to withstand the harshest monsoon conditions year after year.

🏠

Terrace Waterproofing

Crystalline, membrane, and brick bat coba systems for complete terrace protection.

🏗️

Basement Waterproofing

Negative and positive side waterproofing for basements, underground tanks, and sumps.

🚿

Bathroom & Wet Area

Complete waterproofing of bathrooms, kitchens, and utility areas before tiling.

🧱

External Wall Treatment

Water-repellent coatings and sealants for external walls to prevent seepage and dampness.
