Our Story

Built on Trust, Delivered with Precision

BGS Infra was founded with a single mission — to bring honest, high-quality civil repair solutions to residential societies, commercial buildings, and industrial properties across the Thane and Mumbai region.

Over 15 years, we have grown from a small repair contractor into a trusted name in structural strengthening, waterproofing, plastering, and painting — completing over 500 projects and earning the confidence of 2000+ satisfied clients.

Every project we take on is treated with the same level of care and technical rigor — whether it is a single-floor apartment or a large residential complex.

Meet the Founder

Founder & Managing Director · BGS Infra

With over 15 years of hands-on experience in civil engineering, our founder established BGS Infra on the belief that every building — old or new — deserves the best in repair, protection, and care.

Holding a Diploma in Civil Engineering, he brings deep technical expertise in structural assessment, repair methodologies, and project management — personally overseeing quality on every major project.

His philosophy is simple: deliver work that lasts, at a price that is fair, with transparency that builds long-term trust.
